# Question
A triangle is defined in 3D space by the points $A(1, 0, 1)$, $B(3, 2, 1)$, and $C(1, 2, 3)$.

Calculate vectors $\mathbf{AB}$ and $\mathbf{AC}$, compute their cross product $\mathbf{AB} \times \mathbf{AC}$, and use the result to find the area of the triangle.
